About

Joshua Oyewole is a passionate Christian writer and youth revivalist with a deep hunger to see believers walk in purity, fire, and purpose. He is the author of Altars and Patterns, a life-transforming book that uncovers how ancestral altars influence generations and reveals how believers can rise above them through Christ.

Driven by love for God and souls, Joshua’s vision is to awaken hearts, rebuild godly foundations, and equip young believers to carry the fire of revival.
